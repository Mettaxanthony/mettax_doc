Dashcam MD101
=============

-   1-channel 2K dashcam
-   1.09 inch IPS screen
-   Support up to 512GB Micro SD card
-   Built-in Wi-Fi for video view on APP  
    
-   Support motion detection
-   Easy to install with adjustable bracket
